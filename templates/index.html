Embrace the Power of Self-Belief!

Six years ago, I made a bold decision to step out of my comfort zone, take some risks, and dare to dream big.

I faced those familiar butterflies in my stomach, battled anxiety about not doing enough, and grappled with the ever-present fear of failure. These concerns weren't just personal; they were intertwined with a broader purpose and mission aimed at reshaping the current landscape of the trail running and hiking communities.

Throughout this transformative journey, my guiding mantra has been simple yet profound: "Take a bet on yourself." I came to realize that if I didn't have faith in my own abilities and potential, how could I ever hope to effect change or inspire others to believe in their capacity to make a difference?

I invite all members of the Trail Runner Tails community to embrace this empowering mantra, "take a bet on yourself." It's the most potent belief you can hold as you navigate the winding trails of life, pursuing your passions and making your mark on the world.
